The Best Movies According to Libby

My top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

This storyline of this movie is thought provoking and the attention to detail throughout the movie is unmatched.

Shutter Island

This movie is thrilling and has a great plot twist.

Dazed and Confused

This movie is an easy watch and insinuates the nostalgic feeling of being a highschooler in a small town.
